Q. You want to organize documents into distinct groups, without predefining the groups. Which type of machine learning model should you use?
A. Unsupervised learning

Q. You want to explain to a colleague how gen AI works. Which of the following would be a good explanation?
A. Gen AI learns from existing data and then creates new content that is similar to the data it was trained on.

Q. What are some benefits of prompt tuning? (Select 2)
A.
    - Enables large language models (LLMs) to adapt to a wide range of tasks
    - Helps large language models (LLMs) generate more accurate responses
